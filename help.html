📸 Browsing the Gallery

The photo gallery is the heart of the site. Here's how to navigate it:

Filter Tabs

  • All Photos — Shows every approved photo
  • Official — Professional and promoted wedding photos
  • Guest Uploads — Photos shared by family and friends
  • Custom Galleries — Curated collections (e.g. Ceremony, Reception) created by the admin

Hero Slideshow

The large photo at the top of the page automatically rotates through the first 10 official photos with a smooth crossfade effect.

❤️ Liking & Commenting

Liking a Photo

  1. Click the heart icon (❤️) on any gallery photo or in the detail view
  2. Click again to unlike
  3. Your like is remembered in your browser — you won't accidentally double-like

Commenting on a Photo

  1. Click any photo to open the detail view
  2. In the sidebar, enter your name and your comment
  3. Press Enter or click Post
  4. Your comment appears instantly in the thread
💡 Tip

Photos with comments show a 💬 badge with the comment count on the gallery thumbnail.

📤 Uploading Photos

Share your favorite moments from the celebration!

  1. Scroll to the Share Your Photos section
  2. Click the upload area or drag and drop your photos into it
  3. Preview your photos — you can remove (✕) or edit (✎) each one before submitting
  4. Enter your name and an optional caption
  5. Click Upload Photos
  6. Photos are automatically converted to JPG and sent for admin review
⚠️ Note

Uploaded photos won't appear in the gallery immediately — an admin needs to approve them first. This usually happens quickly!

Photo Editor

Before uploading, you can edit each photo with built-in tools:

  • Brightness, Contrast, Saturation — Adjust with sliders
  • Filters — Apply preset looks (Warm, Cool, Vintage, B&W, etc.)
  • Crop — Select a region to crop
  • Rotate — Rotate the photo 90° at a time
  • Reset All — Return to the original unedited photo

📝 Signing the Guestbook

Leave a message for the happy couple! The guestbook supports three types of entries:

Text Message

  1. Enter your name
  2. Type your message in the text area
  3. Click Sign the Guestbook

Audio Message

  1. Switch to the Audio tab
  2. Click the microphone button to start recording
  3. Speak your message, then click stop when done
  4. Preview your recording and submit

Video Message

  1. Switch to the Video tab
  2. Allow camera/microphone access when prompted
  3. Click record and speak to the camera
  4. Review and submit your video message

🔍 Viewing Photos Full-Screen

Click any photo in the gallery to see it larger in the detail view. From there:

  • View the full-resolution image
  • See the uploader's name and caption
  • Like the photo and read/post comments
  • Navigate between photos using arrow keys

🔐 Admin: Logging In Admin

  1. Click the lock icon (🔒) in the top-right corner of the navigation bar
  2. Enter the admin passcode in the modal
  3. The site enters Admin Mode — you'll see additional controls throughout the page
💡 Session Persistence

Your admin session stays active across page reloads. It only ends when you click the lock icon again to log out, or close the browser tab.

✅ Admin: Approving Photos Admin

When guests upload photos, they appear in the Pending Approval section at the top of the page.

Individual Actions

  • ✓ Approve — Adds the photo to the gallery
  • ✎ Edit — Opens the photo editor before approving
  • ✕ Reject — Permanently deletes the photo

Batch Selection

  1. Click the checkbox on individual photos to select them (gold outline shows selection)
  2. Use Select All to toggle all photos
  3. Click ✓ Approve Selected (N) to batch approve
  4. Click ✕ Reject Selected (N) to batch delete (with confirmation)

You can also use ✓ Approve All to approve everything at once.

✎ Admin: Editing Photos Admin

In admin mode, the edit button (✎) appears on all photos except the main wedding photo.

Photo Editor Tools

  • Brightness / Contrast / Saturation sliders
  • Preset Filters — Warm, Cool, Vintage, B&W, Sepia, Dramatic, and more
  • Crop — Draw a crop selection, drag corners to resize, click Confirm
  • Rotate — 90° clockwise rotation
  • Reset All — Revert to the original uploaded photo (from Firestore's originalUrl)
  • Save — Applies your edits and updates the photo in the gallery

Promote / Demote

In the photo detail view:

  • ★ Promote to Official — Moves a guest photo to the "Official" category
  • ↓ Demote to Guest — Moves it back to guest

🗂️ Admin: Managing Galleries Admin

Create curated photo collections for different parts of the celebration.

Creating a Gallery

  1. In admin mode, click the + button next to the filter tabs
  2. Enter a name (e.g. "Ceremony", "Reception", "Getting Ready")
  3. Click Create — a new filter tab appears

Assigning Photos to a Gallery

  1. Click any photo to open the detail view
  2. Use the Gallery dropdown to assign it to a gallery
  3. Select "None" to unassign

Editing / Deleting a Gallery

  • Click the ✎ pencil icon on any gallery tab to rename or delete it
  • Deleting a gallery unassigns its photos — photos are not deleted

Reordering Photos

In admin mode, each photo shows ◄ ► arrows at the top:

  • Click ◄ to move a photo one position left
  • Click ► to move it one position right
  • Order is saved to the database and persists across page reloads

🛡️ Admin: Moderation Tools Admin

Deleting Photos

In admin mode, click the ✕ button on any gallery photo (except the main photo). A confirmation dialog appears before deletion.

Deleting Comments

In admin mode, each comment in the detail view shows a ✕ button. Click it to remove inappropriate comments.

Resetting Likes

In the photo detail view, the Reset Likes button appears in admin mode. This resets the like count to zero.

Deleting Guestbook Entries

In admin mode, each guestbook entry shows a delete button for removing entries.

⚠️ Important

Deletions are permanent. Deleted photos are removed from both the database and cloud storage and cannot be recovered.
